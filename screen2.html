How to Play

The game has 3 rounds. You must win 2 of them to become the winner!

Each round, you must pick between rock, paper and scissors.

The winner of each round is defined by the following rules:

  • Rock beats scissors, but paper beats rock!
  • Scissors beats paper, but rock beats scissors!
  • Paper beats rock, but scissors beat paper!

You have 15 seconds to pick your tool!

When the game finishes, you will be declared winner or loser. Press 'PLAY AGAIN' if you want to give it another try!
